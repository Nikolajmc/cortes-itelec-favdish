James Recipe for the best Spanish Bread Rolls

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (21 / 4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 11 / 4 cups warm water (about 110째F / 43째C)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Instructions:

  1. Activate the Yeast:
    • In a small bowl, combine the warm water and sugar. Stir until the sugar is dissolved.
    • Sprinkle the active dry yeast over the water and let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes foamy. This indicates that the yeast is activated.

  2. Prepare the Dough:
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt.
    • Make a well in the center of the flour mixture and pour in the activated yeast mixture.
    • Add the olive oil to the bowl.
    • Mix the ingredients together to form a dough.

  3. Knead the Dough:
    • Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead it for about 8-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. You can also use a stand mixer with a dough hook for this step.

  4. First Rise:
    • Place the kneaded dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-1.5 hours or until it has doubled in size.

  5. Shape the Rolls:
    • Punch down the risen dough and divide it into 12 equal portions.
    • Shape each portion into a ball or an oval shape, depending on your preference.

  6. Second Rise:
    • Place the shaped rolls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, leaving some space between them.
    • Cover the rolls with a damp cloth and let them rise for another 30-45 minutes.

  7. Preheat the Oven:
    • Preheat your oven to 375째F (190째C) during the last 15 minutes of the second rise.

  8. Bake:
    • Bake the rolls in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes or until they turn golden brown.

  9. Cool:
    • Allow the Spanish bread rolls to cool on a wire rack before serving.
